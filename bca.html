Bachelor of Computer Appliction

Tribhuvan University

Bachelors: 4years

The BCA program under the faculties of humanities and social sciences of Tribhuvan University aims to produce skilled computer application users and developers. It spans four years, is divided into eight semesters, and is offered in >130 colleges with 4500 seats for enrollment. The medium of instruction and examination of BCA is English.

ELIGIBILITY

Passed in 2081 B.S
  • Minimum D grade in each subject of grade 11 and 12 with a CGPA 1.8 or more
  • Minimum score of second division marks in 10+2, PCL, or equivalent in any discipline.
  • Students who have passed grade 11 and are waiting for grade 12 results can also apply. However, they have to submit all the required documents at the time of admission.
