Tribhuvan University:Bachelor 4years

Tribhuvan University has launched four-year bachelor's course in BA from the academic year 2076/77 under the Faculty of Humanities and Social Science.

The four-year course is of 2000 marks (500 marks each year) and students can choose two subjects of 700 marks each as their major and two subjects of 100 marks each as elective subjects.

There will be three compulsory subjects- English, Nepali and Nepal Studies. English will be of 200 marks, Nepali 100 marks and Nepal Studies 100 marks. English has been made compulsory in first and hird year, while Nepali will be compulsory in the second year.

ELIGIBILITY

Passed in 2080 B.S
  • Student should have secured at least D+ in all subjects in Grade 11 and 12.
  • Must have complete the intermediate, proficiency certificate or 10+2 level, in Humanities or Science or Management stream from Tribhuvan University or from Any other University or Board organized by TU.
